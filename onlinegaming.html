ONLINE GAMES

that I play are..

Minecraft is a game made up of blocks, creatures, and community. Survive the night or build a work of art. But if the thought of exploring a vast new world all on your own feels overwhelming, then fear not!

The world of Sky: Children of the Light explores a fantasy-based Kingdom, using free-roaming experience and a magical Cape that grants players the ability to fly and flap. There are seven unique Realms to visit, and each one is themed around a different stage in life.

Roblox allows users to program and play games created by themselves or other users.

Project Zomboid is an open-ended zombie-infested sandbox. Survivors must loot houses, build defences and do their utmost to delay their inevitable death day by day.

Escape the Backrooms is a 1-4 player co-op horror exploration game. Traverse through eerie backrooms levels while avoiding entities and other danger to try and escape.

Supermarket Simulator is a chill first-person simulation where every detail of running a supermarket comes alive. Design your store, optimizing for efficiency and aesthetics. Determine where products are displayed, manage your aisles, and ensure a smooth shopping experience for your customers.
